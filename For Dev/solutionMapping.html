Attendee Barriers – “Lost Young Adults”

Mapping

1.1 Fragmented Platforms & Processes

AttendeeBarrier
  • Sub-Issues: Multiple disconnected sites, inconsistent UX, no central calendar, brand inconsistency.
  • Why Critical: Young adults lose confidence; discovery depends on who they already know.
  • Impact: Missed events, duplicated effort, weak first impressions, uneven quality.

  • Ministry Impact: More youth feel welcomed sooner; easier to invite friends; fosters unity.

Outcome: Consistent, stable foundation for growth.
