Project Overview

RisingGen solves critical problems facing European Young Single Adults by creating one unified platform that makes event planning, community connection, and meaningful participation simple and accessible.

Critical Pain Points We Solve

🗓️ Event Chaos

YSAs struggle to find events across multiple channels, leading to poor attendance and missed opportunities for connection.

🤝 Isolation

Young adults feel disconnected from their peers across Europe, limiting faith-building friendships and support networks.

📚 Information Overload

Important updates and uplifting content get lost in the noise of multiple communication channels.

⚡ Service Coordination

Organizing service projects and meaningful actions requires complex coordination across multiple platforms.

How RisingGen Navigation Works

🗓️

Events

Discover and register for YSA events across Europe - conferences, activities, and gatherings all in one place

🤝

Community

Connect with other YSAs, build friendships, find mentors, and create lasting faith-building relationships

📚

Content

Access uplifting content, stay updated with announcements, and engage with meaningful discussions

⚡

Service

Organize and participate in service projects, share your testimony, and make a meaningful impact

👤

Login/Signup

Secure church member verification ensures a safe, trusted environment for all participants
